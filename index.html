Projects

Density

Density shows how full popular study spaces are at Columbia. Density is also an API that gives access to Wi-Fi data to Columbia developers. It is currently maintained by ADI Labs, a program I co-founded to develop apps for Columbia.

Stack: Python/Flask/PostgreSQL.

Switch

Switch helps Columbia students find other Columbia students to switch classes with. Students create listings that include their current class information and classes that they want to switch into. Switch sends an e-mail to students that have matching listings.

Stack: Python/Flask/MongoDB.

Instafeedback

Instafeedback allows you to get feedback from an audience instantly. You can create sessions and poll your audience to see how many people are confused. Audience members can also ask questions online that will appear instantly to the presenter. Instafeedback was intended for teachers to gauge their students comprehension of the material.

Stack: Node.js/Express/MongoDB/Socket.io.

Impetus

Impetus is a tool for Columbia students to find other Columbia students with similar interests. When students find a match, both students receive an introductory e-mail to each other along with the interests they have in common.

Stack: Node.js/AngularJS/MongoDB.
